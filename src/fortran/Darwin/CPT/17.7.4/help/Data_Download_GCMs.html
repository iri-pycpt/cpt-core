Download: GCM Outputs

All the GCM outputs are model hindcasts and real-time forecasts, and so are meant for use in predictive analyses and forecasting (there are no AMIP-type outputs, for example). Access is provided to outputs from eight of the National Multi-Model Ensemble (NMME) members, and five Copernicus Climate Change Service models (C3S). These models and their model identifier (for batch version users) are:
  • NMME models:
    • CFSv2 (ID 11): with hindcasts starting in January 1982. (Start dates between January and July 2019 are unavailable.) A non-NMME version of the model (ID 12) is available.
    • CanCM4i (ID 33): with hindcasts starting in January 1981. (Start dates between January and July 2019 are unavailable.)
    • CanSIPSv2 (ID 34): with hindcasts starting in January 1981. (Start dates between January and July 2019 are unavailable.)
    • COLA RSMAS CCSM4 (ID 41): with hindcasts starting in January 1982.
    • GFDL CM2p1 aer04 (ID 54): with hindcasts starting in January 1982.
    • GFDL FLOR A06 (ID 52): with hindcasts starting in March 1980.
    • GFDL FLOR B01 (ID 53): with hindcasts starting in March 1980.
    • NASA GEOSS2S (ID 72): with hindcasts starting February 1981. (Start dates between February 2017 and November 2017 are unavailable.)
  • C3S models:
    • ECMWF SEAS5 (ID 101): with hindcasts starting in January 1981. (Start dates between January and August 2017 are unavailable.)
    • Meteo-France System 7 (ID 111): with hindcasts starting in January 1993. (Start dates between January 2017 and September 2019 are unavailable.)
    • GloSea5 System14 (ID 121): with hindcasts starting in January 1993. (Start dates between January 2017 and April 2019 are unavailable.)
    • CMCC SPSv3 (ID 131): with hindcasts starting in January 1993. (Start dates between January 2017 and October 2018 are unavailable.)
If multiple pressure levels are selected, CPT will read these as multiple fields. Otherwise, to combine predictors and / or models, first download the individual files and then use the Data ~ Merge option. The data are typically updated towards the end of the first week of each month.
