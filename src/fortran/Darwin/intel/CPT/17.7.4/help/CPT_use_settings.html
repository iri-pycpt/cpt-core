Program Settings

Once the X and the Y files have been opened successfully, CPT is ready to construct a model. However, it may be advisable to adjust some of the program settings first. Some of these options may be prompted as you open the files. Important settings to consider are:
  • Setting the domain
  • X and Y modes
  • CCA modes
  • Training data
  • Replacing missing values
  • Regression options
  • Transforming the data
