547: Exclude

To exclude specific observations from the analysis, uncheck the radio buttons against the date(s) to be excluded. Any excluded dates will be omitted from the training period, as well as from any climatological calculations. It is not possible to exclude dates from a climatological period that extends outside of the training period.

The Reset button sets all dates to be included.
