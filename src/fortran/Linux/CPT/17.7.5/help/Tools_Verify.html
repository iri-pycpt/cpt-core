430: Verification

Verification scores and graphics can be obtained for the retroactive forecasts or the double cross-validated hindcasts. Specifically, attributes (or reliability) diagrams, ROC graphs, tendency and ranked-hits diagrams, and some "Weather Roulette" graphics (cumulative profits and effective interest rate graphs) can be drawn, and a suite of probabilistic scores calculated. Exccept for the skill maps, all results are calculated from pooling all the locations together, and are based on the probabilistic hindcasts (evaluation of the quality of the deterministic hindcasts is available from the Validation) menu item.
